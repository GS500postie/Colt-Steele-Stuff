Literally taken me hours building the navbar, with chatgpt even. This is a very effective exercise; the more I do this the quicker I will get. Also learned a couple of things about the inspector and using other tools. I can't stress enough how this is a very valuable way to approach web design and the more I do this the quicker my trajectory will be. I was reminded today: you can't ride a bike by looking at videos or doing courses; you only learn by getting on the bike and riding, and you only get better at it the more you ride. I know it's ruining the web page but I have to jot down these thoughts because I think I'm onto something. The more I do this the easier it will get. Learning and courses will still have a place, I just have to spend the majority of my time, or at least a large chunk just banging my head against the wall. This is how I learn.
